About me

I live in London and work as a data engineer at ASI Data Science. I am a core contributor to Jupyter widgets and the main author of jupyter-gmaps, a Python library for visualizing geographical data on Google Maps.

I have recently completed a PhD in computational physics at Cambridge, working on quantum Monte Carlo methods.
