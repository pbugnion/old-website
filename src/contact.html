About me

I am a software engineering technical lead or team lead. I build tools for data science and machine learning. I live in London and work at Faculty, as part of the Faculty Platform team.

Outside of my day job, I am a contributor to open source, specially in the Jupyter and Plotly ecosystem. I am a core contributor to Jupyter widgets and the main author of several Python libraries.

I hold a PhD from Cambridge University in theoretical solid state physics, working on quantum Monte Carlo methods.
